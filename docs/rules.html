Rules

Proposed by Paul Spetrini for the 2026 season.

1. Keepers

  • 2 max keepers.
  • Can keep a player twice, for a 3-year total lifespan.
  • Cost to keep increases 1 round from initial draft pick each year.
  • To keep an undrafted player: give up a 7th round pick at first, then 6th.
  • Keeper trade does not reset the keeper clock.
  • Keeper eligibility is round 5 and onward.

2. Trade Lockups

  • Can trade any players through the week before playoffs.
  • Cannot trade or acquire round 1-2 future picks.
  • Veto committee will be in place. TBD on who exactly will be on it and when.

3. Waiver

  • Switching to budget system.
  • $100 allocated to each manager per season.
  • Minimum of $1 per claim.
  • Tie on a claim goes to the manager with fewest points for.

4. Reset

  • Instead of a full reset: fixed order draft for rounds 1-4, then snake draft from round 5 onward, with order based on last season's ranking.
  • Rationale: most impactful picks awarded to those well-positioned for this year's draft; later picks smooth out the order for more even picks.
  • This is a compromise — not a full reset or no reset.
